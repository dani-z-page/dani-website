The point of view

Who is buying fills the funnel. Who is succeeding determines whether growth holds.

Marketing sees who responds. Sales sees who will buy. Product sees what active users do. Customer Success hears from established accounts. Support sees customers at the moment of friction.

Each view is useful. None is the whole customer. I connect those signals to identify which customers succeed, why others fail to realize value, and what the company should target, build, change, automate, or stop.

Otherwise, new logos simply backfill churn—and the topline calls it growth.
