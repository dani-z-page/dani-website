From product complexity to 67% better conversion

Situation

A PLG SaaS company had weak trial conversion and a long list of proposed fixes.

What the evidence showed

Customer interviews, activation drop-off data, and support patterns converged on one central issue: non-technical customers struggled to build successfully before they could experience meaningful value.

What changed

I proposed and drove an AI-guided product experience and rebuilt onboarding around validated customer use cases and intent.

Results

—AI-guided product adoption increased 3×
—Trial-to-paid conversion improved by 67%

From a broad customer motion to stronger NRR

Situation

A SaaS customer organization was operating reactively while market pressure increased retention risk.

What the evidence showed

The company was applying the same service motion too broadly rather than concentrating high-touch effort on customers with the strongest fit and expansion potential.

What changed

I rebuilt segmentation, engagement models, and customer plays around the journeys and economics of the highest-potential ICP segments.

Results

—NRR increased 30% in twelve months
—Expansion revenue grew 23% YoY
—NPS increased 15 points
—High-touch and scalable motions were separated

From customer research to a new enterprise product

What changed

Results

—New enterprise product and revenue lane
—Larger, more efficient expansion opportunities
—23% YoY expansion revenue growth
—NPS of 54
